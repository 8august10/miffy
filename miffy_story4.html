Miffy's Holiday Adventure

Miffy was playing in the snow with her friend, Boris Bear. Suddenly, they heard some bells ringing in the distance. They followed the sound through the snowy woods, exploring new places. They found some colorful presents left under a big tree and some birds eating berries. Miffy was very curious about everything she saw and was delighted by her holiday adventures. Miffy is always open to new experiences.
